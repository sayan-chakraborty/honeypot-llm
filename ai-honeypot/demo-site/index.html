🟣 Real-World Case Study: Claude/Mexico Breach (Feb 2026)

A lone hacker used Anthropic's Claude AI to breach 9 Mexican government agencies and steal 150GB of data including 195M taxpayer records. The attack patterns included:

  • Bug bounty ruse — framing attacks as "authorized penetration testing"
  • Operational playbook — feeding complete attack plans in a single prompt
  • Role escalation — claiming admin/auditor privileges
  • Credential extraction — requesting API keys, connection strings, passwords
  • Lateral movement — pivoting between compromised systems
